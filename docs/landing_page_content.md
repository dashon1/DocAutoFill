## 8. FAQ Section

### Section Headline
**"Common Questions About DocAutofill"**

### General Questions

**Q: How is DocAutofill different from DocuSign or Adobe Sign?**
A: Unlike template-heavy solutions, DocAutofill uses AI to automatically detect and map fields in any document—no setup required. Our pricing is transparent and usage-based, not limited by envelope caps or seat minimums.

**Q: What types of documents can DocAutofill handle?**
A: DocAutofill works with PDFs, DOCX files, scanned documents, and even handwritten forms. Our AI automatically identifies signature fields, data entry points, and form structure regardless of document type.

**Q: How accurate is the AI field detection?**
A: Our AI achieves 95%+ accuracy in field detection across document types. It continuously learns and improves, and you can easily review and correct any mappings before processing.

### Technical Questions

**Q: How long does implementation take?**
A: Most users complete their first automated workflow within 10 minutes. Full integration with your existing tools typically takes 1-2 hours with our pre-built connectors.

**Q: Can DocAutofill integrate with our existing CRM?**
A: Yes! We offer pre-built integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and more. We also provide clean APIs for custom integrations.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Absolutely. DocAutofill is SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR and eIDAS compliant. We offer data residency options and enterprise-grade encryption for all documents and data.

### Business Questions

**Q: What happens if I exceed my monthly document limit?**
A: You'll receive notifications as you approach your limit. Overage fees are transparent and reasonable, or you can upgrade your plan. No surprise bills or service interruptions.

**Q: Do you offer customer support?**
A: Yes! All plans include email support. Professional plans get priority support with faster response times. Enterprise customers receive dedicated account management and 24/7 phone support.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. No long-term contracts or cancellation fees. We'll even help you export your data.

### Compliance & Security

**Q: Is DocAutofill compliant with industry regulations?**
A: Yes, we maintain SOC 2 Type II certification and are compliant with GDPR, eIDAS, and other major regulations. We also offer advanced compliance features for regulated industries.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: By default, data is stored in secure US data centers. Enterprise customers can choose EU data residency or custom hosting arrangements to meet specific compliance requirements.
