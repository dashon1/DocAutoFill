# Target Audience Research Plan for Document Automation

## Research Objective
Analyze target audiences for document automation focusing on pain points, demographics, and value propositions for:
1. Individuals with frequent form filling needs
2. Small businesses
3. Government form filers  
4. Professionals who handle paperwork regularly
